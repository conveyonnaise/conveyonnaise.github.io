Lilian held the main mechanical design task for Conveyonnaise. With no background knowledge of Solidworks, Lilian managed to complete the proposal drawing within three days. Starting from a young age, Lilian has been interested in Math and Physics. At the age of 20, she is enrolled in the toughest program UBC offers, Engineering Physics, and will be completely a Minor in Commerce upon her graduation. With the free time she has on her hands, she develops applications for personal projects. Lilian is also a passionate lifeguard and aquatic fitness instructor for the City of Burnaby.
